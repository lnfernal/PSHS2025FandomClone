Undertale Alternative Universes

Undertale Alternative Universes(AUs), are variations on an original universe, in this case, Undertale.

Types

There are two types of AUs, namely,

Alternative Universes

and

Alternative Timelines

Alternative Universes

completely deviate from the parent universe, in the sense that the characters, the setting, and the universe is different



ex. Underswap, Storyshift, Underfell, Rubytale, Deltarune, etc...


Alternative Timelines

, on the other hand, occur in the same universe. It consists of the same characters. The part where it deviates is in the timeline. Different set of events lead to different outcomes.



ex. Glitchtale, Undertale YELLOW, etc...
