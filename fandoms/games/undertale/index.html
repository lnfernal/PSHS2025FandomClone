Undertale

Undertale is an RPG game created by developer Toby Fox. It is a unique RPG experience, because unlike other RPGs, you don't need to kill monsters to finish the game. In fact, you are encouraged to use the mercy option repeatedly.



Plot

2 millenia ago, humans and monsters lived peacefully on the earth. But then, humans became scared of the monsters' ability to absorb their souls. This fear slowly led into a war. The humans won the war and sealed the monsters underground using the power of seven human souls.

Development

Toby Fox originally set-up a fund-raising for Undertale. He originally set a goal of 5000$, but he got back an amount more than 10x of his goal.
