Hobbies

Lauren

loves writing stories. In fact, she created an entire Undertale Alternative Universe called Wondertale. She has a habit of killing off a considerable percentage of her main characters. And so if you find yourself in one of her stories, then you can try one of the following things:

  • Develop Velocity Nine so that you can run fast enough to distort the space-time fabric around you, thereby creating Einstein-Rosen bridges to escape from your reality.
  • Make a particle accelerator explode on you, get struck by lighting, and inject chemicals used in forensic sciences upon yourself. A side effect of this is that you finally have the ability to travel at relativistic speeds such as 0.9999999c. Using this newfound power, run around in a circular fashion in order to create a wormhole. To stabilize the wormhole, you would need to find exotic matter. These are usually found in vacuums. And then, use tachyons to enter the wormhole as any velocity less than or equal to c cannot make it through the inner ergosphere of the rotating wormhole. Once you have made it through, you would've made it in a different sector of the universe, free from the killing of characters.
  • Spill paint on the grass and call Ink Sans. Once he arrives, ask him to take you to the doodle sphere. Then, enter any other universe.
  • Induce desperation upon yourself, this will get the attention of CORE!Frisk. CORE!Frisk will probably take you to the Omega Tim--[REDACTED].
  • Create An Aperture Science Handheld Portal Device in order to create Einstein-Rosen bridges. Fire a portal on a piece of moon rock and wait for a test subject from one of the infinite universes which contains an Aperture Science Testing Course. Go through the portal. Once you have reached the Aperture Science Testing Course, try escaping GLaDOS, a sentient AI who has neurotoxin which can kill you in a matter of microseconds. A good strategy would be to replace GLaDOS' cores with defective ones. Once defeated, try not to be taken back into the facility by a party escort bot.


She also draws a lot on Ibis Paint X. Like Gabee, she programs a lot, but on scratch instead of combined web languages.
