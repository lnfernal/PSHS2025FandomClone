Rubytale

Rubytale

is an Undertale Alternative Universe originally conceptualized by Gabee De Vera(RedBlazerFlame), Sarah Veloso(SarahVeloso6606), Lauren Valencia(MagicalMyu), and Craig Adan(TDArmy).



The AU is the same as undertale, except that students from 7-Ruby(2025) and Undertale are swapped. The AU is storyswap in nature.



Premise

"It was like we were sucked into the videogame. We had to ‘Play’ as these characters." -Gabee De Vera

Characters from Undertale and People in Ruby are matched in groups of 2. Each group includes one Undertale Character and one Ruby Student.

Development

RubyTale

was originally conceptualized to be an encryption method. If we need to talk to someone about someone else, we substitute the name of the ruby student for the Undertale Character.



Ships

The only canon ship in both RubyTale and 7-Ruby would be Marla x Iel(Alphys x Undyne). As for past ships, Sarah x Sean(Frisk x Sans), can be considered.
